Wicker Park Crossfit is a 1,300 square foot full service Crossfit gym. The space includes a 44 foot rig of racks and pull-up bars, Concept 2 rowers, barbells, bumper plates, gymnastic rings, kettle bells, plyo boxes, medicine balls, and olympic weightlifting

WPCF is a big box mentality in a small group setting. Our coach to athlete ratio ensures each athlete receives personal attention and quality training.
